# Auto Detailing Naming — Owner Summary

**Carlsbad, CA · Funded fixed-location shop, open to rebrand · 2026-06-06**

A business name is a *findability and word-of-mouth tool* — it gets you onto the list;
your reviews and service win the customer. Pick a name that's easy to say, spell, and
Google, that signals "detailing," and that stands out from a market full of look-alike
"Carlsbad ___ Detailing" and "mobile detailing" names.

## Recommendation

- **If Carlsbad is home for the long haul → "Official Carlsbad Detailing" (OCD).** Most
  memorable + strongest local search; the "obsessive about detail" wink is perfectly
  on-brand. (Trade-off: ties the name to Carlsbad if you ever expand.)
- **If a second location is possible → "Facet Detailing" or "Crisp Detail Co."**
  Distinctive, easy to say/spell, trademark-friendly, no geographic lock.
- **DTYL** (because it *sounds like* "detail") works **only as "DTYL Detailing"** — it's
  brandable, but it's the hardest to spell, say aloud, and find by voice search. A
  sentimental pick, not the safe one.

## Do

- Make **"Detailing" part of your real, signed brand name** (register a DBA so it's legitimate).
- **Lean into the storefront** — most local rivals are mobile; a real shop is your edge.
- Use the **exact same name** on your sign, website, Google, and Yelp.

## Don't

- **Don't keyword-stuff** the Google name or tack on a tagline — it risks suspension.
- **Don't be another "Carlsbad ___ Detailing"** — that lane is saturated and forgettable.
- **Don't let the name carry the business** — reviews win the click; the name just gets you seen.
